ABOUT ME

Proactivity, determination and creativity are the motors
that push me to always give the best

Hello, let me present you, my name is Raffaele Aveta, I’m 25 years old and I’m from Italy, from Caserta, a city near Naples. My two biggest passions are connected among themselves, in fact they are technology and most specifically web development. Since I was a young boy I’ve always been attracted to all the world that goes around these two main things, most of all because it’s amazing to think how life can be (and it is) better just with the help of something that’s on the internet or something that is made by technology! I mean it’s insane where life is going and where the world is going! When I was a child my parents gave me as a present a programming language book, and since then it was “love at first line!” (as it’s usual to say in technical words). Thanks to all that, I’ve started my web developer full stack studies, going also through web design. All that because I truly believe that a good web developer should know both aspects of the web to be able to do his job at 360 degrees. A basically example can be the importance of knowing both the back and front end, the first to fully understand how servers and websites interact among themselves, and the second to develop designs and ways to make easy to use a website for all the people, everywhere. So to flank my web developer formation, as I already said, there are also web design studies because I’m truly interested in giving to my clientele all the best experience they can have and also totally interact with my teamwork. I’m also an art enthusiast in general, in fact my academic career is made by an art high school diploma and lots of certificates, always regarding arts. For example I’ve done a tattoos and piercings course, that allowed me to totally express myself and my personal art, most of all because with this work you have to be fully connected with the other’s minds to be really connected to what they really want. As it’s easy to deduce by these lines, I’m a dynamic person, I really love to better myself with every single thing is under my possession. I love and I want to continue to improve my mind and my cultural experience, filling it by concepts and technological skills, just to make everything of that available to every company I work with. At the completion of my skills there are a good inclination to teamwork, a really good relational quality, thanks to my many works experiences that formed me, and also a marked organizational skill that obviously is always essential. The major softwares I use are Git, Visual Studio Code, WordPress, Photoshop, Lightroom, Sketchbook. I give the best of me in active contexts and I feel comfortable in difficult circumstances, they give me the strength to always give the best.
